What is Expell?


Expell is an interactive social network based on the interaction between users with their most recent experiences. Also it offers the possibility of evaluating the experiences of other users and commenting on them to develop a suitable environment for all people who love to travel and to live incredible experiences.

Expell emerged as a small startup in 2018 started by a group of young people from the company Techy that would quickly take shape and materialize in 2020. At this point, Expell began to welcome its first users and the team expanded rapidly, willing to meet the needs of the volume of active users.
