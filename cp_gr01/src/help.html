Expell FAQ


How to register in Expell?

Welcome to Expell, we are so thriving you want to join our family. In order to registrer, you need to check the upper-right button and fill the form with required data. You will be prompted to fill in your name, email address, a password which you have to keep secret and the URL of any image you like for your profile. Once completed these steps, you will be able to enjoy all the advantages of a full category user!


How to upload an experience?

First of all, to updload an experience you should be logged in. When you are logged in, you may go to the 'Experiences' section and click the 'upload experience' button. Then you will be required to add the photography of the experience and provide a description for all people to read it.


How to log in?

In order to log in, you have to clic on the upper-right button and fill the form with your account credentials. Unfortunately, have you forgotten your password, you will not be able to recover it due to security reasons.


How to activate dark mode feature?

Simply check the upper-right button and select the 'dark mode' option :)
